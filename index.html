About ISTE Chapter

WE ARE HERE TO BRING CHANGE!! Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education in the field of engineering and technology registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860. Established in the year 1941, it aims at the Career Development of Engineering Teachers and Personality Development of Students, contributing to the overall development of our Technical Education System. The ISTE Chapter at our college is a vibrant and dynamic community of students who are passionate about technology and innovation.

Our Mission

Empowering India's Tech Education

Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation and excellence in technology education. By fostering collaboration among educators, facilitating knowledge exchange, and promoting innovative teaching methods, we aim to elevate the standard of technical education across India.
